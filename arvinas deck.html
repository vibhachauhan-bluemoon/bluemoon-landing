Biological Structure Does Not Follow Diagnosis

  • Diagnostic labels mask true biological similarities
  • Patients organize into distinct geometric clusters
  • Clusters are highly mixed across clinical labels
  • The same biological structure appears across diseases and tissue types

Biological Position Maps to Mechanism Compatibility Across Domains

  • Subtypes occupy distinct regions of mechanism space
  • Position defines compatibility with underlying biological programs

Non-Response Is Structured, Not Random

  • Low compatibility indicates mechanism mismatch
  • Mismatched patients can be rerouted via relative score (ΔS)
  • Alternative mechanisms map directly to unserved patients

Implications for Mechanism-Driven Development

  • Explain structured non-response in trials
  • Evaluate new mechanisms before clinical deployment
